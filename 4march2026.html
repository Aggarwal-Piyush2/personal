For You

A LITTLE PIECE OF MY HEART

I want you to know something clearly. I want to come to you. I have no problem coming. The only reason I sounded hesitant is because people around me keep telling me to reconsider because of the situation happening in the world. It made me pause and think more carefully, but it never changed how I feel about you. You are not a maybe in my life. You are someone I chose. Distance, opinions, fear — none of that changes the way I feel about us. What we have matters to me. And I'm not going anywhere.
